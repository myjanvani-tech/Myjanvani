Dinesh Tiwari
Editor, My Janvani

Dinesh Tiwari is a senior media professional and development expert with over 21 years of experience in journalism, media management, livelihood development, CSR, and government coordination.

He has worked with reputed organizations including Ministry of MSME (Govt. of Uttar Pradesh), Rashtriya Sahara, Allahabad Express, Tata AIG Life Insurance, and Indian Society of Agribusiness Professionals (ISAP).

He has strong expertise in media management, public affairs, stakeholder coordination, fundraising, skill development, and community empowerment. He has actively contributed to rural development, women empowerment, and financial inclusion programs across Uttar Pradesh.

As Editor of My Janvani, his mission is to provide reliable news, highlight social issues, and give voice to the people while maintaining journalistic integrity and transparency.

Location: Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh
Languages: Hindi & English
Experience: 21+ Years
